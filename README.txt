Team Members: Dominic Beninati (dbenin3), Nikita Srinivasan (nsriniv)

App Description: Users can use Mixology to search for a general type of drink they would like, and save all
resulting drinks, required ingredients, and recipe instructions. Users can also filter the saved drink list 
for a specific drink by filling out a form to indicate their desired filters.
